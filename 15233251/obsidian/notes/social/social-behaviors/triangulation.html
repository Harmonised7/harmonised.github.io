triangulation

A more complex and severe form of victim complex, where the manipulator involves one or more third-party, often with heavy use of deliberate omission of relevant information via willful ignorance, downplaying their own responsibility in the matter via deflection, and deflecting the responsibility upon the target.

triangulation usually starts with involvement of closest people to the manipulator, who are most likely believe them, and play along as their (fake) rescuer - often emotionally blackmailing them into cooperation.

Purpose

The purpose is to recruit allies to take their side, fueling their victim complex, enhancing their power play, and leaving you helpless and powerless in the situation, with others on the manipulator's side.

taking things public pressures the target, as the situation has been escalated without their consent, potentially against their preferences.

It also puts the target into a disadvantage due to the intentional omission of truth and relevant context.

It also pressures the target into feeling cornered, by threat of feeling outnumbered.

The manipulator also assigns the victim as the target for the abuse that the manipulator is claiming to have been put through. This pressures the target into feeling like they need to defend themselves against the false accusations, that the third-parties are not aware are false, due to the omission of truth by the manipulator.
