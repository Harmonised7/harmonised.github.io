Willful Ignorance

Deliberate Omission of Information that would prove you incorrect, and Hoping that anyone else involved doesn't catch onto it either, so that you could Feel/Be Perceived as correct.

Willful Ignorance is always harmful. It is a Gaslighting technique.

It Expresses "If I don’t Acknowledge it, it’s not real.", Dismissing any Legitimate Concerns.

  • Sometimes it's mild, like a child that's adamant on being unable to learn something new - suddenly realizing that they learned something new.

    • The child could now Willfully Pretend that they in fact did not learn anything, that it's impossible, that the people who claimed that they could learn were wrong, and may even take Fitful steps to "Prove" that they did not - even if it's blatantly obvious for the older kids/adults that they are throwing a Tantrum.

  • And sometimes it's incredibly Harmful, when used as a Manipulation Tactic, to Willfully Ignore someone's Efforts, such as an Expression that would either resolve/solve the Conversation/Argument, or as little as prove that the Willfully Ignorant person is incorrect.

    • The Willfully Ignorant person would not Acknowledge the parts that are Problematic to their point, suggesting to the Target that either the Willfully Ignorant person simply does not understand and may not deserve to be Responsible for the entire Blame due to their lack of Understanding, or that they should put in even more Effort into explaining now, becoming further Exhausted, only for it to be wasted once again, and be further Willfully Ignored, and less likely to catch onto further attempts at Manipulation, due to lack of energy.

Willful Ignorance also appears as a person that has been Manipulating you and giving you a hard time - suddenly treating you especially friendly, perhaps even Defending you, where you didn't even need Defending - only in Hopes of being Perceived as your Ally, completely Ignoring all the Drama that they have caused while Manipulating you. This is a form of Gaslighting, as you are tempted to question your Perception of the Situation, and it's especially difficult now because you are aware that if you bring up the Abuse/Manipulation caused by the Manipulator - you will come off as looking for trouble/causing Drama, so you are more likely to endure the Gaslighting instead, perhaps even give into it and start questioning your Perception "Maybe they're actually a nice person, and I am the one who is Dramatic, and trying to cause trouble out of nowhere. That's so Immature of me".
