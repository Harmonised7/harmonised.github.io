stonewalling

An emotional blackmail power play manipulation tactic that ensures that the manipulator has the final word, which is most often another manipulation tactic intended to gaslight the target, as they are made unable to respond to the message/sentence by the manipulator. This is an attack from within the Victim's head that is intended to simultaneously rile up the victim, and render them powerless in the situation, with hopes that they also start feeling that way. It is a cruel manipulation tactic.

The victim will now likely feel anger/fear/guilt/responsibility that the attacker intended them to feel.

the victim's sense for justice will likely start looking for a target to act as an outlet to the injustice that they have been put through.

With the manipulator no longer being available for communication - it's more than likely that the closest person that would understand the injustice, and the psychology/mental processes/emotion/emotional processes/struggle that comes with bearing it is the victim themselves.
Depending on their mental fortitude and emotional maturity - the victim may start fighting the image of the attacker in their head now.

Afterall, who could blame them? injustice was thrown their way, and it is their obligation to defend their image, as no one else will! How dare this person accuse me of these unfair things? They're such a jerk!!!

The problem is that an image of anyone in your head is still you. Fighting an image of yourself in your head: is fighting yourself. This is exactly the intent of the attacker - for you to eat away at yourself, with their attack as the seed for all the terrible emotions.

stonewalling is a mental parasite:

  • If you choose to ignore it: Chances are likely that it will keep on festering inside you until you can no longer ignore it. Until it gets out of hand.
  • If you have the mental skills to combat this manipulation tactic safely and choose to do so: It will still require your mental and emotional energy.

stonewalling prevents closure, ending things completely on the attacker's terms.

Examples

  • "you need to understand how i feel or else there is no way i will understand how you feel, this goes both sides and you keep forgetting that goodbye.... miss (Name)..."
    • emotional blackmail, suggesting (and hoping you believe it yourself, too) that the stonewalling was caused solely by you - that you were so stubborn that you couldn't get over yourself (Promotes feeling guilt) and have left them with no choice but to block (stonewall) you, which they express through reluctancy, again suggesting that they are stonewalling you with a heavy heart, like your relationship was so important to them.
      • The missing context here being that the person has been manipulating you this entire time, while you kept your composure, and behaved respectfully. Unlike them.
