Interpretation

- Process

The way we Process new information, Situations, and Experiences, based on our already existing Knowledge ( Memory ), Beliefs, and Perceptions.

- Skill

An ability that can be Learned, which allows using your Skill to make more accurate and Insightful Conclusions from new information and Behaviors.

This skill often utilizes Critical Thinking, and Empathy.

In Volatile fields such as Politics, Interpretation is futile due to extreme Unpredictability.
To attempt Interpretation in such a Chaos is Naive and Fooling, because each second is a uniquely complex Result of countless Processes beyond anyone's Comprehension.
