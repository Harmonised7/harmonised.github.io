Victim Complex

A Manipulation Tactic where a fake image of being a Victim, with Intent to lessen Accountability and/or Danger/Consequences towards the (Fake) Victim.

Victimization is extra Dangerous, because Conscious Intent is not required.
Some people learn to play the (Fake) Victim role instead of dealing with their Responsibilities in a healthy manner, and over time truly believe that they are a real Victim.

Victimization of self may be used as either means of protection (mental or physical) or acquiring benefits, such as better treatment, Perception, or better chances at winning an Argument.

The (Fake) Victim first assigns a (Fake) Persecutor. The recipient of their Accusations. The person being Manipulated.

It works because people don't want to hurt others, and when they are assigned the Persecutor role, it might make them Feel Guilty, Insensitive, Cruel, or even Attacking an "Innocent" (even if fake) (Fake) Victim, which will make them question themselves if perhaps they truly are hurting an Innocent person, and they're the bad person (aka successful Manipulation by the (Fake) Victim, that gives them an advantage).

The (Fake) Victim may also seek a (Fake) Rescuer (Enabler, someone to Vouch for the Credibility of their (Fake) Victim claims), who's purpose is to Enable the (Fake) Victim by Validating their Immoral Views, and Vouching the (Fake) Victim's Victimhood to others, giving them extra Credibility.

This makes the Situation extra tricky, because it can still be Effective, even if the person being Manipulated is aware of this Tactic, because even if they are aware of it, a different person that is not aware of the Manipulation may fall for it instead, Perceiving them as Guilty, Insensitive, Cruel, or even Attacking an "Innocent" (Fake) Victim. This allows for Non-Verbal Blackmail between the (Fake) Persecutor, and the (Fake) Victim, that other people are Far less likely to catch up onto.

It's even more Effective if the person being Manipulated is not aware of the initial Tactic, as they will now be tempted to question their own Deduction of the (Fake) Victim being a (Fake) Victim, and start questioning themselves - doing the (Fake) Victim's work for them - Gaslighting themselves into believing that maybe this actually is a true Victim, and that they have been doing a terrible Mistake.

It can make the Manipulated person feel Uncomfortable in pursuing their original Accusations/Demands, whether they were reasonable, or not.

Victimization Manipulates people into giving them an advantage, by being given more Compassion, Leniency, and Generosity, sometimes even resulting in an Apology and/or compensation for their troubles, from the person that is being Manipulated, and is in the right, unlike the (Fake) Victim.
