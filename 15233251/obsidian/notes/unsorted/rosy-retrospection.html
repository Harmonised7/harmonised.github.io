Rosy Retrospection

Recalling Events more Positively than they actually Occurred, "Through Rose-Tinted glasses".

  • This is done by our Minds for all Memories, including Memories that were:

    • Positive
      • How In Awe you were when you first met someone you Love very much right now, the first moment you saw them.
        • in Reality: you did Recognize that person immediately, more than anyone else, However, I doubt you were immediately In Awe. The Truth is that you have just Met the Person and Lacked the Information you know now that makes you Remember it so much more Rose-Tinted.
          • Rosy Retrospection does not Diminish the True Events, nor the Memories. It is a Wonderful Process that our Minds Utilize to Cherish, Be Grateful For, and Appreciate the people that are Important and Loved in our Lives even more and keep them Close to us and make sure they are Safe, Protected, and Happy <3
    • Neutral
      • How Fun and Enjoyable a Mundane task was
        • In Reality: It wasn't particularly Enjoyable, nor was it Tedious. It was Mundane. Perhaps 90% of the task was Spent doing the same thing over and over.
          • Yet we Remember it being better than it was, because it was Useful to us. It Accomplished a task that wouldn't normally do if there was no Reward attached to it. It's a great Motivational Technique our Minds Utilize!
    • Negative
      • How much you Learned and how Useful those Skills and Information from your Education were.
        • In Reality: it was actually an incredibly Inefficient, Tedious, and Stressful Wasteful of your Time, Energy, and Resources. Perhaps even your Emotions and Mental Health, perhaps you even Suffered Trauma from that Experience, yet your Recollection of that period of Time tells you differently, even though you know it's Wrong.
          • This is Useful for us because it Helps us to Accept and Forget the past, to leave it behind, and Moving On, instead of Dwelling On The Past, and it's Injustices, and getting nowhere in Life. We are Resilient! We Learn, we Move On, and we Blossom~ <3

  • Being Aware of Rosy Retrospection does not only not Diminish the Positive Effect and Usefulness, but it actually Enhances it! It Allows us to look at things in Retrospect, and Appreciate things, bringing us Gratitude <3

    • For Negative ones:
      • It Allows us to Recognize how much we have Moved On, how Insignificant our Pain and Suffering from the Past is now, in the Present. How our Present lacks all that Awfulness, and gives us Gratitude that we have something so much better now <3
    • For Neutral ones:
      • It gives us immense Pride and Admiration of ourselves, of our Dedication and Persistence to Accomplish such Seemingly Crazy and Difficult tasks. Imagine! Spending hundreds, perhaps even thousands of hours Studying subjects you find Interest in, for your own sake alone, without the Ability or even the Desire to Brag, be Admired, or even Recognized. Only for the sake of Learning, Growing, and Improving yourself as a Person. Yeah, who'd do that! ;p
    • For Positive ones:
      • It Shows us who had stuck by our side in our Lives, through the Difficult times, and the Wonderful times. Who were there for us and Helped us when we had no one else. Who Cared for us and gave us Kindness. Who we will always Cherish and who we will never, and can never Forget and will always Love, Cherish, and Appreciate <3
