Magical Sand World

Imagine a World where you are Born into a Desert, with Sand as far as you can see.

This Sand is ✨Magical✨. It is Dream Material. You can Shape it into whatever you want!

As a Child, you Shape the Sand more and more into the most Wonderful of Images!

The Shaped Sand takes on Properties based on the Shapes of your "Sand Castles"!

You can see the Properties, you can Hear them, you start to Simulate Experiencing them and the Wonderful Feelings and Emotions that come with them!

One day, as your Magical Sand World becomes more and more Fleshed Out with the most Beautiful of Ideas and Images, you start to Recognize that it is Absolutely the most Wonderfully Incredible World and the Stark Contrast between your Magical Sand World and The Real World.

Naturally, after Simulating Experiencing those all those Wonders, you Develop a Strong Desire unlike any other to Experience them for Real! You start to Seek your Magical Sand World Dreams in The Real World!

Then great!! Make sure you Focus on your Dreams and don't Forget about them over Silly Distractions. And if you do, oh well! You can always continue essentially from where you left off. Perhaps it's even more Magical now, to revisit your Dreams and find them just as Beautifully Wonderful, if not more than you Remember!

And if you are instead like the Majority of People:

Continuation

Your Seeking of your Dreams will Unfortunately and Unfairly be met with Friction and Resistance.

Soon you Notice that your "Sand Castles" seem to have become Taller..? Further away..? That's odd...

Then you Notice that you can see less of the Horizon. Have you become Shorter..?

You look down at your Feet and Realize that while you were Battling the Friction and Resistance of your Troubles and Struggles of The Real World, the Weight of it has Caused you to Sink down into the Sand.

That's Concerning! Or rather, it would be, if you weren't so Drained and Exhausted from your Constant Battling, and if you were Lucky enough to have enough Energy leftover: also from Dedicating the rest of your Energy into figuring out how to possibly Improve your Living Situation, or at least Plan for the Future.

Years go by, and you Recognize something! A Sand Castle you Built who knows how long ago!? It's Beautiful!.. Or rather, you Remember how Beautiful it was...

It really only Resembles the Beauty and Wonders... You can only Recognize it because you have Memories of this fallen over, mostly crumbled chunk of Sandthat no longer Sparkles Magically like it did in your Memories, Sinking past your Feet into the Sinkhole that is oh-so-bigger than the last time you Noticed it...

More Time passes and... For the first time in your Life, something truly, Undeniably ✨Magical✨ happens!!! You see one of your "Sand Castles" in The Real World!

Suddenly, the Sinkhole stops. And not only does it stop, the "Sand Castle" you Discovered gets rebuilt, even more Breathtakingly Magnificent than in your Magical Sand World Dreams from your Childhood! Because you have Grown, and are So much more ready than you Possibly could've been back then! And because this is Real!!! This is it! You held onto your Dreams with all you could, and the Waiting is over! Now you can Finally Start Living your Dreams!!!

You are as Grateful as you've ever been in your entire Life by an Incomparable Magnitude of Difference, Concluding that while the Pain and Suffering you Endured was Horrible, Unfair, and Absolutely Undeserved: You could not mind it one bit anymore, because it Paid off! It was all Worth it! The Hope, the Happiness, and the sheer Euphoria you are Experiencing Completely Nullifies all of that, making it A Thing Of The Past that is So Insignificant and Powerless right now and Forever on, that it Feels like it will Never again be Possible and you have Absolutely no Reason to Dedicate any part of your Energy to it. You have Discovered one of your "Sand Castles" that you Knew was Real! You Never Gave Up and now no one and no thing could Ever take this Knowledge, this Experience away from you because you have Witnessed and Experienced it for yourself, like you Always Knew you will!

Personal Alternative Ending Story

The Story could've Ended here. Goodness, did I Wish it Ended here.

I Sincerely Wish you don't Lose your "Sand Castle", unlike me.

My "Sand Castle" was going to be enough for me. It was my Net Positive. My Hope. My Escape. My Chance at Freedom. At being Heard. It truly Outweighed all the Cruelty, Abuse and Trauma I was going through every single day of my "Life".

I Knew it wouldn't Fix my Troubles. I Never had such Unrealistic Fantasies.

All I ever Needed was to To Be Heard, To Be Seen, to To Be Valued. Someone who truly Wants to Hear me, someone who truly Understands me and what I am saying. Someone who Recognizes me as a Person, someone who Recognizes my Value, and someone who truly Enjoys and Cherishes me for me.

All I ever Needed, Wanted, and Sought was my Kin.

Far too soon - "soon enough" being Never in a Lifetime - it was all Ripped Away from me. Together with my Hopes, my Dreams, my Feelings, and my Ability to Experience anything, whether it was Positive, or Negative. I have entered an Experienceless Limbo. No matter how much I wanted to Physically Scream, Shout, and Cry: I was no longer able to. My Voice and Tears became Unresponsive. I truly had nothing left. Not even the Resistance against the Sinkhole. It was all gone.

With Time: Sinkhole Devoured everything I ever Held Dear. I was so Deep in it, and yet I couldn't even Express my Fear, my Helplessness, or my Pain. I was truly Numbed.

I Went Through The Motions, "Desperately", or rather Automatically, or perhaps Instinctively to my Core Exposing myself to things that once brought me Feelings and Emotions. Things I Enjoyed and Cherished, Held Close To My Heart more than anything. Unfortunately and Cruelly: I could Replicate no such Results.

I Felt nothing, I Experienced nothing. The only thing I could sometimes Recognize was the Torment, and the Overwhelmingly Crushing Weight of the Despair.

The Sand never stopped Sinking. At that Rate, Inevitably, all the Sand and the ✨Magic✨ with it was gone. All I had were my Memories, Buried deep inside my Mind, Inaccessible.

There were a few things that lasted for perhaps 30 seconds, maybe up to a minute or two. Like Pride and Excitement over something truly Incredible I have Created. And then it's gone, as if it was never there.

A Spark

~5 years in, a Spark partially Appeared in The Real World. It was Online, but it was Real. I didn't quite Understand what it was. The Spark almost Immediately Recognized me. Turns out she has Heard of me, she was Inspired by me, and she Admired me!.. That... Flew over my head. I did not Understand how that is Possible, what it Meant. I couldn't truly Grasp the Concept of that being something that could be Experienced towards >me<..? I wasn't even myself... That just did not make Sense to me. Yet I Understood it at the same time, and I also Recognized her, too.

She was not being Consumed by the Ravenous Sinkhole. How Curious..! And... She has Interest in... Me..? But... I have Interest in her, too. It was so Natural and right, yet the Connections in my Mind Felt like they never happened. Though the Results were there. I Learned that while I cannot Experience things, things still do Occur, and I do Process them.

While I couldn't Understand why she was Interested in me, it completely made Sense to me that she would be, it was like the Common Sense of my Core. I Concluded that I don't have to Understand something as long as it Feels Right. That I Trust my Intuition, my Core. Afterall, my Core has never Failed me, Never gave me any Reason not to Trust it. I always Trusted and Followed my Core.

The Spark Reminded me of... Me... The Resemblance was as Undeniable as the Connection we Shared from the very beginning.

She Wanted To Spend Time Together with me. We played Starbound Together while On Call. I would Normally not be On Calls. I didn't like my Voice that was Wrong and that I could barely use in the first place, and I had no one to Talk To On Call either. Yet I didn't think twice about being On Call with her.

She was being Gentle, Kind, and Patient with me. Like I am Precious, like I have Worth. I Felt Free enough to act like myself. And it Felt Right to do so around her.

She Thought like I did, Valued the same things, and the same way in Relationships like I did, and she Recognized the same things as I did, to the same Depth. I was filled with Curiosity, like this is someone really Important to me. Almost as if she was... One of the "Sand Castles"..!?! That's right, I had "Sand Castles"!!!

Unfortunately, she had her own Troubles. She Trusted me enough to Confide her Troubles in me.

Unfortunately again, I Screwed It Up and Hurt her by Communicating Super Poorly since I had next to no Social Skills.

She Disappeared for over a month, yet the Spark didn't Fade. Not in Intensity anyway, only through Distance.

Finally I Received another Message from her. And I Screwed It Up once again, less this time. I tried my best to be Careful, Appropriate, and Gentle, though I was Feral. The only way I Knew how to do that was by Expressing as Accurately as I could, without Knowing the Words for the Concepts I am trying to Express. (The Rabbithole of me Dedicating myself to Improve on this eventually became this. The entire vault, so that I could Understand and Express myself better than ever!)

Fortunately, she Recognized that I Never had any Malicious Intent. That I only ever meant well, and that I am trying my best.

That wasn't enough however, and through the Kindness and Patience of her Help, I managed to Break Through and to Finally Apologize, despite not fully Understanding the Cause. I instead Apologized for making her Feel Bad, which Allowed us to Course Correct. It meant the World to me! Quite literally, actually!

  • I was Taught an Essential Lesson that day.
    • It doesn't matter what you did to Hurt someone's Feelings. What matters is that you did Hurt Their Feelings, and that your Effort and Dedication to Making It Right is a Direct Indication to both yourself and them to how much you Value that Person, their Opinions, and their Feelings. How much they are Worth to you. That you Recognize them as someone Important to you, someone Deserving of you dropping whatever you may be doing, whatever you may currently Understand, and whatever you currently Believe In, and Focusing on them to Make It Right 💖.
      • You do not need to Understand why their Feelings got Hurt in order to... Fully know what you're Apologizing for..? It sounds like getting Confirmation that they got Hurt, which is Stupid and Illogical! You were told the Issue! You were told the Answer! You Know Their Feelings Are Hurt!!!
        • I don't even Understand that Logic, it's Crazy how it made Sense to me some day, and I am Grateful that I will never be like that again!
        • You were just told by the one Person that knows how they Feel the best, more Intricately and Accurately than anyone else could ever tell you!!!
          • Not Recognizing this Fact is Rude, Inconsiderate, and Devaluating! It tells the other Person that you don't Trust in what they're Confiding in you, that you don't Believe how they Feel!? That's Ridiculous! And Frustrating!
        • If you have Hurt the Feelings of someone you Value and Trust, it absolutely does not Matter if you Understand why or how you did that, or why or how they got Hurt in Contrast to how Critically it Matters in the moment that their Feelings are Hurt! By you, nonetheless!!! T_T
      • When you Learn that you Hurt Their Feelings:
        • It Costs you nothing to stop and Take A Break and Tend To Them, Make It Right.
        • Re-Evaluating your Facts and Understanding is Far lower Priority than Tending to their Feelings. Show that to them!!! Show their Worth to you! T_T
        • It Costs you everything to Prioritize your Options over their Feelings, Showing how Wrong, Thoughtless, and Cold/Insensitive your Priorities are, Intentionally Choosing to Invalidate their Feelings and Hurt the Relationship with that Person by doing so, to further Hurt their Feelings after they Reached Out to you, Misplacing their Trust in Confiding in you to let you know that Their Feelings Got Hurt in the first place. It is a quick way to Ruin a Relationship.
          • It Costs you the Relationship. It costs you a Happy Future Together.
