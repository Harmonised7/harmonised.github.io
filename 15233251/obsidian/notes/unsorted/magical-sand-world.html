magical sand world

imagine a desert world with sand as far as you can see.

This sand is ✨magical✨. It is dream material. You can shape it into whatever you want!

As a child, you shape the sand more and more into the most wonderful of images!

The shaped sand takes on properties based on the shapes of your "sand castles"!

You can see the properties, you can hear them, you start to simulate experiencing them and the wonderful feelings and emotions that come with them!

One day, as your magical sand world becomes more and more fleshed out with the most beautiful of ideas, images, and experiences you start to recognize that it is absolutely the most wonderfully incredible world! And the stark contrast between your magical sand world and the real world.

naturally, after simulating experiencing all those wonders, you develop a strong desire unlike any other to experience them for real! You start to seek your magical sand world dreams in the real world!

Personal Alternative Ending story

I sincerely wish you don't lose your "sand castles" or your sand altogether, like I did mine.

My "sand castle" was going to be enough for me. It was my net positive. My hope. My escape. My chance at freedom. At being heard. It truly outweighed all the cruelty, abuse and trauma I was going through every single day of my "life".

I knew it wouldn't fix my troubles. I never had such unrealistic fantasies. With my net positive: I could finally breathe and see a future. I couldn't ask for more.

All I ever needed was to to be heard, to be seen, to to be valued. Someone who truly wants to hear me, someone who truly understands me and what I am saying. Someone who recognizes me as a person that I am, someone who recognizes my value, and someone who truly enjoys and cherishes me for me.

All I ever needed, wanted, and sought for was my kin.

Far too soon - "soon enough" being never in a lifetime - it was all ripped away from me. Together with my hopes, my dreams, my feelings, and my ability to experience anything, whether it was positive, or negative. I have entered an experienceless limbo. No matter how much I wanted to physically scream, shout, and cry: I was no longer able to. My voice and tears became unresponsive. I truly had nothing left. Not even the resistance against the sinkhole. It was all gone.

I went through the motions instinctively to what felt right to my core, exposing myself to things that once brought me feelings and emotions to see if anything can help me. Things I enjoyed and cherished, held close to my heart more than anything. unfortunately and cruelly: I could replicate no such results.

I felt nothing, I experienced nothing. The only thing I could sometimes recognize was the torment, and the overwhelmingly crushing weight of the despair.

New sand would come, at a lesser and lesser rate, yet the rate of the sinking was overwhelming. The sand levels were at a net negative. At that rate, inevitably, all the sand and the magic with it sunk away. All I had were my memories, buried deep inside my mind, inaccessible in this negative state of mind.

There were a few rare feelings and experiences that lasted for perhaps 10 seconds, maybe up to a 30, or even a minute. Like pride and excitement over something truly incredible I have created. And then it's gone, as if it was never there. They were like bonus sand that carried some ✨magic✨ with it. Yet only a little bit. No match for the sinkhole.
