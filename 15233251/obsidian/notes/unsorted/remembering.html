Remembering

  • We Remember Feelings and Emotions most Effectively and Accurately Compared to fine detail, words, images, etc... For all of which we Summarize instead. But not Feelings/Emotions.
    • When we Remember a Feeling - we have no Doubt that we Recognize it.
    • We can even Recognize Feelings we either Feel incredibly Rarely, or have last Felt an incredibly long Time ago, or have never Felt before. Yet it Makes Sense to us and is Natural to us.
      • Like Experiencing Love and Kindness after not having any for most of our Lives, or ever so Genuinely and truly.
    • Yet we can Walk a path we've walked on before, perhaps even numerous times, Hear a Concept we've Heard before, hear a Word we've Heard before and Think it's the first time we are Experiencing, and not Think much of it. Can even swear we never Experienced it, because it is not as Intuitive and Essential to us.
    • Evolutionarily: Feelings and Emotions are one of the most Primitive concepts that Sentient Beings (like us Humans) can Experience.
      • We've had them for far longer than we had Thinking, Reasoning, or Conscious Thoughts in general.
      • They are so incredibly Core to us; we Understand them incredibly Intuitively.
      • Feelings and Emotions are like the lowest level Language of all Sentient Beings.
        • This is part of why Trauma is so Effective, and so incredibly Difficult to get rid of, especially without the right Mental Tools.
          • Words are an incredibly Powerful Tool for Sapient Beings (like us Humans). Yet we Developed these Tools Far after we Developed Feelings and Emotions, which makes them not as Effective/Intuitive as Feelings and Emotions for us to Understand Intuitively, which are far more at our Core of Experience and Understanding.
            • We can Feel and Experience just fine even when we are at a bad enough State to be unable to Think properly.
