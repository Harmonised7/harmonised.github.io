Backhanded Compliment

An Insult Maliciously Disguised as a Compliment.

Examples

  • "That's pretty Smart, for someone without an Education/your age."

    • Attack on the Person's Education level, or age.
      • Sincere Alternative: "Wow, that's really Smart! If I didn't know, I would've thought you: graduated from a university/you're older than that!"
        • Lack of Education and being young are used to Emphasize the Impressiveness of their Smartness!

  • "You're Lucky to be Loved by someone so good, despite all your Quirks."

    • Attack on the Person's Quirks.
      • Sincere Alternative: "It's great that you found someone who Loves you and your Quirks, it really shows how Sincere their Love is towards you!"
        • Their Quirks are instead Portrayed as a Positive Trait, which Compliments the Sincerity and Genuinity of the Love! How Kind!

  • "For a beginner, you did a good job."

    • Shifting the Focus of the Person's Accomplishment to the Fact that they're a beginner, Rudely Diminishing the Compliment part.
      • Sincere Alternative: "You did a good job, especially for a beginner!"
        • The Focus of the sentence is now the good job, which comes first - and them being a beginner only Emphasizes their Accomplishment! How Sweet!

  • Backhanded Compliments Shift the Focus from the Insincere Compliment into an Insult, using the Compliment to create Plausible Deniability for the Insult, making it more Elusive, to a point where the person being Insulted may either be Gaslighted into Believing that they are just Imagining the Malice since it is "Obviously a Compliment" or simply not Recognizing the Insult in the first place and be Sincerely Grateful towards the Person Insulting them.

    • This makes Backhanded Compliments especially Nasty, usually used as an Abuse of Power against those that are:
      • Aware of the Nastiness but cannot Defend themselves due to a Power Imbalance
        • A boss Backhandedly Complimenting an Employee and the Employee having no Realistic Choice but to Pretend that they are not Aware of the Disguised Insult and Respond Positively to their boss. A Disgusting Abuse of Power and Power Imbalance.
      • Unaware of the Nastiness, to the Enjoyment of the Person Insulting, where they Humiliate the Person without them even Knowing.
        • Older, Smarter kid or adult Backhandedly Complimenting a younger, less Aware kid or adult.
